# China Market Entry for Electronics and Hardware Buyers

> **Read the full guide**: [https://china-sourcing-agents.com/guides/china-market-entry/](https://china-sourcing-agents.com/guides/china-market-entry/)

How to enter the Chinese electronics supply chain: supplier selection, factory audits, compliance, and first-shipment strategy for hardware buyers.

---

Entering the Chinese electronics supply chain for the first time takes 4–6 months if you plan it. It takes 12–18 months if you don't. The gap is almost entirely in three decisions made in the first 30 days: which supplier tier you target, whether you audit before ordering, and how clearly you define specifications upfront.

This guide is written for hardware startups, Amazon FBA sellers, and European or US distributors taking their first serious step into Chinese electronics sourcing. It covers the full sequence — from market mapping to first shipment — with the practical details that most "intro to China sourcing" articles skip.

The phrase is used two ways. One meaning: a foreign company entering China as a consumer market (setting up a WFOE, hiring local sales, localizing products for Chinese consumers). That is not this guide.
