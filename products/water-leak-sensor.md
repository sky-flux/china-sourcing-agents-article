A water leak sensor detects standing water via conductive probes and fires a local buzzer plus a push alert — and, in a well-designed system, triggers a smart valve to shut off the supply. For Gulf homes with concealed plumbing, the value is the **alarm linkage** (does it actually close a valve?) and a **loud local buzzer** that works even if the cloud is down. With a radio and a battery, the Saudi market needs CST approval and a UN 38.3 report. This page covers water leak sensors sourced from China for KSA.
