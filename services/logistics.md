Logistics is where deals go wrong after everything else went right. An incorrect HS code adds 25% tariff. Missing battery documentation grounds a shipment for 3 weeks. A factory that books the cheapest forwarder ships your goods via 3 transshipment ports with no tracking.

We've seen all of it. The fix is not becoming a freight operator — it is selecting the right China freight forwarder for each shipment and controlling the chain from factory gate to your warehouse door, whether you ship to the US, EU, or Japan. For EU startup logistics on a 5,000-unit Bluetooth speaker delivery, we coordinated sea freight door-to-door with zero customs holds. A Japanese distributor shipment of LoRa gateways similarly cleared customs without delays after we ensured all FCC and CE documentation was in order ahead of time. We handle consumer electronics shipments routinely and know exactly which documentation pitfalls to head off for battery-powered and RF devices. We work with 3–4 vetted forwarders in Shenzhen and Dongguan. For most shipments, we get competitive quotes from 2–3 of them. We handle booking, pickup scheduling, and cargo handoff documentation.
