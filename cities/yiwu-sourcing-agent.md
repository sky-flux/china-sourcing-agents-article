Yiwu International Trade Market (义乌国际商贸城) is the world's largest wholesale market — 75,000 shops across 5 zones, 210,000+ buyers visiting annually from 200+ countries. For electronics accessories under $5/unit, Yiwu sets the global wholesale price floor. USB cables, phone cases, charging stands, LED string lights, power bank shells — if it's a commodity accessory, Yiwu is where it starts. The Yiwu–Europe railway (direct freight trains, 18 days to Germany) has made Yiwu one of the most significant nodes in cross-border e-commerce logistics, extending its reach beyond its traditional wholesale role.

**Consumer electronics accessories** are Yiwu's core category for electronics buyers: USB-A/C cables, MFi-compatible cables (certification verification required), screen protectors, phone cases, wireless chargers, portable battery shells, earphone accessories, Magsafe-compatible mounts, multi-port USB hubs, LED digital clocks, and quartz watches. Pricing on standard catalog items is typically 20–40% below equivalent Alibaba listings because you're buying direct from the wholesale market, not from a trading company markup.

**Small household electronics** — LED strip lights, smart plugs, decorative lighting, USB desk fans, multi-port adapters, and basic smart home accessories — are concentrated in District 2 and District 3 of the International Trade City. Products at this level carry basic CE/RoHS declarations and are primarily destined for Amazon FBA, Shopify stores, and European retail distribution.
