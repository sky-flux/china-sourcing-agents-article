A door/window contact sensor is a two-part magnetic reed switch that reports open/closed state — the volume backbone of any home-security system. The two variables that separate a reliable OEM unit from a return-generating one are **gap tolerance** (how far the two halves can separate before triggering) and **honest battery life**. Because it contains a radio and a coin cell, the Saudi market needs CST approval and a UN 38.3 battery report. This page covers door/window sensors sourced from China for KSA.
