China's LED lighting industry is split into three clusters with different strengths. Zhongshan, and especially Guzhen, makes finished fixtures — bulbs, panels, downlights, and decorative lamps — at scale. Shenzhen and Dongguan produce LED drivers, modules, and smart-lighting electronics. Foshan and the Pearl River Delta round out the supply chain with raw materials and components.

If you are sourcing LED lights, the first decision is which cluster matches your product. A Zhongshan factory can deliver a finished ceiling panel at low cost, but it may source the driver from Shenzhen. A Shenzhen factory can build a smart RGB system, but it may not be the cheapest source for a plain white downlight. This list covers both finished-fixture manufacturers and module specialists. Specific product categories we source include LED strip lights, smart ceiling lights, smart downlights, LED track lights, and all-in-one solar street lights.
