Jiangmen sits at the western edge of the Pearl River Delta, 75 km from Guangzhou and 110 km from Shenzhen. While less internationally recognized than Dongguan or Shenzhen, Jiangmen holds a globally significant position in one specific industry: motorcycle manufacturing. One in five motorcycles exported from China originates here, with total motorcycle export value exceeding 21 billion yuan. For buyers in the two-wheel vehicle or small engine categories, Jiangmen is the primary destination. Partnering with an expert Jiangmen sourcing agent ensures rigorous factory verification, certification documentation review, and strict quality control monitoring for motorcycle and small engine procurement programs.

The motorcycle cluster concentrates in Pengjiang district. Dachangjiang Group, Tayo, and JMZF are among the largest manufacturers, but the real depth of the cluster is in the supply chain beneath them: engine block foundries, transmission gear manufacturers, brake system assemblers, electrical harness producers, instrument cluster factories, and plastic body panel injection molders.

This vertical integration is what makes Jiangmen genuinely competitive for motorcycle OEM and private-label programs. A buyer developing a branded motorcycle or electric two-wheeler for the European or Latin American market can procure the complete bill of materials within Jiangmen, streamlining supply chain management. Engine displacement ranges from 50cc scooter power units to 400cc+ adventure bike engines.
