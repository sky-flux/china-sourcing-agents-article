Outdoor solar string lights have become an essential product category for landscape lighting, patio decor, and garden aesthetics. As a buyer looking for a reliable solar string lights OEM wholesale manufacturer in China, understanding the technical specifications—such as battery chemistry, solar panel efficiency, and wire durability—is critical to avoid high return rates.

Battery chemistry selection in outdoor solar string lights involves a genuine tradeoff between cost, safety, cold-weather performance, and shipping logistics — not a simple upgrade path. When sourcing solar lighting products from China, this is the first specification to lock down.

NiMH (nickel-metal hydride) cells have been the default for consumer solar garden lights for two decades. A 1.2V / 1200mAh AA-format NiMH cell costs $0.45–0.75 at volume. The chemistry handles overcharge more gracefully than Li-ion — a solar charge controller failure that delivers excess current to a NiMH pack results in heat and reduced cycle life, not thermal runaway. For outdoor lights left unattended through seasonal variations, this safety margin matters.
