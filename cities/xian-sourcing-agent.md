# Xi'an Sourcing Agent — Samsung NAND, Aerospace & Rail

> **Read the full guide**: [https://china-sourcing-agents.com/cities/xian-sourcing-agent/](https://china-sourcing-agents.com/cities/xian-sourcing-agent/)

China sourcing agent in Xi'an: Samsung NAND supply chain, aerospace precision electronics, Shaanxi agricultural products, and China-Europe rail freight.

---

Xi'an is Western China's most consequential electronics sourcing city — driven not by sheer factory volume (Chengdu and Shenzhen exceed it on that measure) but by the specificity of its industrial capabilities. Two forces define the Xi'an supply chain: the Samsung NAND flash fabs that have seeded a semiconductor-grade manufacturing ecosystem in the High-Tech Zone, and the aerospace and defense electronics heritage that predates the Samsung investment by 50 years. For overseas buyers, these two legacies produce a cluster of suppliers qualified to process standards rarely found in inland Chinese cities.

Beyond electronics, Xi'an is a genuine sourcing destination for Shaanxi's agricultural exports — jujube, kiwifruit, apples, walnuts — and serves as the primary rail freight hub for the Changan-hao China-Europe railway, one of the highest-frequency rail services running between China and European ports.

Samsung Semiconductor Xi'an operates two NAND flash wafer fabrication plants in the Xi'an High-Tech Industrial Development Zone, representing one of the largest single foreign investments in China — cumulative capital exceeding $26 billion across two phases. Samsung's own products are not commercially sourceable from these fabs.
