## What is China Sourcing Agent?

[China Sourcing Agent](https://china-sourcing-agents.com/) is an **engineer-led sourcing agency** helping companies worldwide source electronics, IoT modules, smart home devices, and PCBs from verified Chinese manufacturers. Unlike traditional trading companies, our team has hardware engineering backgrounds — we read schematics, evaluate BOMs, and verify factory capabilities against actual equipment, not sales presentations.

**Core services**: [Sourcing & Supplier Matching](https://china-sourcing-agents.com/services/sourcing/) · [Factory Audit & Verification](https://china-sourcing-agents.com/services/audit/) · [Quality Inspection](https://china-sourcing-agents.com/services/inspection/) · [Private Label & OEM](https://china-sourcing-agents.com/services/private-label/) · [Logistics Coordination](https://china-sourcing-agents.com/services/logistics/)

**Serving**: Hardware startups, Amazon FBA sellers, industrial IoT integrators, and distributors across North America, Europe, Middle East (Saudi Arabia, UAE, GCC), and Japan.
