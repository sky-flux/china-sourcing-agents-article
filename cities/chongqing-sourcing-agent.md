# Chongqing Sourcing Agent — Motorcycles, Vehicles & Rail

> **Read the full guide**: [https://china-sourcing-agents.com/cities/chongqing-sourcing-agent/](https://china-sourcing-agents.com/cities/chongqing-sourcing-agent/)

Engineer-led China sourcing in Chongqing. Motorcycle capital (7.8M+/year), Changan Auto base, Yangtze port and China-Europe railway (13 days to Duisburg).

---

Chongqing is China's undisputed motorcycle capital. The numbers are not rhetorical: in 2025 this single municipality produced 7.8 million motorcycles, representing over one-third of China's national output. Loncin, Lifan, and Zongshen — three of China's largest motorcycle manufacturers by global export volume — are all headquartered here. The downstream ecosystem includes 50+ vehicle assemblers and over 400 component suppliers within the municipality, manufacturing everything from bare engines and gearboxes to instrument clusters, lighting assemblies, and electronic fuel injection systems.

Beyond motorcycles, Chongqing is also a major four-wheel vehicle manufacturing center (Changan Auto is headquartered here), a growing furniture cluster, and — critically for logistics — both the starting point of the Yuxinou China-Europe railway and the site of China's largest inland river port on the Yangtze.

**Motorcycles and two-wheelers** are Chongqing's defining manufacturing category. The city's three major brand anchors — Loncin (隆鑫), Lifan (力帆, now under Geely's ownership after restructuring), and Zongshen (宗申) — have created a complete vertical supply chain that independent buyers can access at the OEM/ODM level. Buyers developing private-label motorcycles, branded commuter bikes, or specialized off-road models have access to complete motorcycle development from design specification through tooling, certification, and export preparation. MOQ for complete motorcycle builds at established assemblers: 300–500 units. For buyers needing smaller quantities for market testing, CKD (completely knocked down) kits with local assembly are a standard arrangement.
