The E40 LED retrofit lamp replaces HID (metal halide, high-pressure sodium, mercury vapour) lamps in existing industrial and street-lighting fixtures without changing the housing. The E40 Goliath screw base is the standard for high-wattage luminaires across Europe, the Middle East, and South/Southeast Asia. Our sourcing service helps you identify factories with genuine thermal test data and verified driver reliability — not just paper specs — and our industrial IoT expertise extends to evaluating power supply quality for large-scale lighting deployments.

The commercial case is straightforward: a 40W LED retrofit delivering 7,200 lm replaces a 250W HID lamp that produces 22,000 lm — but the HID fixture at end-of-life is often running at 50–60% of rated output, making the actual replacement ratio closer to 1:1 in perceived brightness. Combined with 6× lower power draw, retrofit payback periods of 18–36 months are typical in 3,000+ hour/year industrial applications.
