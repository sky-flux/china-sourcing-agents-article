# Robotic Lawn Mower OEM China: Wire-Free, CE, Factories

> **Read the full guide**: [https://china-sourcing-agents.com/blog/robotic-lawn-mower-oem-china/](https://china-sourcing-agents.com/blog/robotic-lawn-mower-oem-china/)

Robot mower shipments grew 63.8% YoY. Chinese factories now produce wire-free RTK models. What European and US buyers need to know before sourcing.

---

Robotic lawn mower shipments grew 63.8% year-over-year in 2025. Wire-free models — the ones that navigate using RTK GPS or vision-based boundary detection instead of a perimeter wire — now account for 66% of shipments. Chinese manufacturers drove most of that growth. Segway (Ninebot) and Mammotion (Agilex) are shipping wire-free units into the US and European markets at price points that repositioned what $800–1,500 buys. Behind them is a second tier of ODM factories that most Western brands have not found yet.

This is what the sourcing picture looks like if you want to put your brand on a wire-free robot mower.

The perimeter-wire model has been around since Husqvarna's Automower launched in 1995. Installation requires 2–4 hours of wire-laying, a professional installer in most European markets, and a $150–300 service call every time the wire breaks. For a product that costs $800+ at retail, a required $200 install is a conversion killer.
