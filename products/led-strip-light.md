LED strip lights are one of the most counterfeited and spec-inflated product categories in China. When sourcing LED strips for smart home or architectural applications, key things to verify:

**Actual watt draw vs. rated.** Measure actual power consumption with a power meter on a sample reel. Budget strips frequently draw 60–70% of rated wattage because fewer LEDs are actually populated per meter or the current per LED is underdriven. Always verify electrical specs during quality inspection.

**Copper PCB vs. aluminum PCB.** 24V high-density strips (≥14W/m) need good heat dissipation. Check the PCB copper weight — 2oz copper is significantly better than 1oz for thermal management. Request the PCB spec sheet.
