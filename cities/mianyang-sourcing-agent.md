# Mianyang Sourcing Agent — Changhong Electronics & LED Mfg

> **Read the full guide**: [https://china-sourcing-agents.com/cities/mianyang-sourcing-agent/](https://china-sourcing-agents.com/cities/mianyang-sourcing-agent/)

Engineer-led sourcing in Mianyang. Changhong OEM ecosystem, industrial LED makers, and smart-home/IoT suppliers from Sichuan's national science city.

---

Mianyang sits 90 minutes north of Chengdu by high-speed rail and carries the formal designation of China's only national-level science city outside Beijing. That designation traces to the Third Front Construction movement of the 1960s, when strategic industries and research institutions were relocated inland. The China Academy of Engineering Physics — China's nuclear weapons research institute — was established here, and the defense research cluster that followed created a city with engineering talent density well above what its size would otherwise produce. For electronics sourcing, the practical significance of that history is a civilian manufacturing ecosystem anchored by one of China's oldest electronics brands, a growing LED manufacturing sector, and component suppliers with genuine engineering capability operating in the shadow of one of China's most research-intensive urban environments.

Changhong is the organizing fact of Mianyang's electronics industry. Founded in 1958 as a military electronics factory and converted to consumer electronics production in the 1980s, Changhong became one of China's largest TV manufacturers and has since expanded into air conditioners, refrigerators, and an IoT platform business under the CHiQ brand. Its Mianyang headquarters campus is one of Sichuan's largest single manufacturing sites.

For international buyers, Changhong's relevance comes in two forms. The first is direct OEM access: Changhong operates a commercial OEM program for flat-panel TVs from 40-inch to 100-inch, connected home appliances, smart display products, and IoT gateway hardware. Buyers developing private-label electronics in these categories can engage Changhong's OEM commercial team for production programs, with MOQs typically starting at 500–1,000 units for TV programs and 1,000–2,000 units for PCBA subassemblies. CE, FCC, and RoHS certifications are standard on Changhong's export product lines, reducing the certification burden for buyers who can use Changhong's existing test reports as a baseline.
