Standalone or networked RFID access controller supporting Mifare 13.56MHz and EM4100 125kHz cards. Wiegand 26/34 output, IP65, CE/FCC certified.

---

Access control is a long-lived, safety-critical system — a failure that locks people out of a building is a serious operational problem — and a core component of any smart building or security infrastructure. Our sourcing service helps you identify manufacturers with proven reliability records. Specify for reliability, not just price.

**Card technology.** EM4100 (125kHz) is a legacy, clone-vulnerable technology — anyone with a $30 RFID cloner can copy an EM4100 card in seconds. For any serious application, specify Mifare Classic, Mifare DESFire EV2, or LEGIC Advant. DESFire EV2 is the current recommended standard for secure access.

**Relay rating and fail-safe vs. fail-secure.** Specify whether the door lock should fail open (fail-safe — door unlocks on power loss) or fail-locked (fail-secure — door remains locked). This is a fire safety consideration in many jurisdictions and must match the door hardware. Verify the relay current rating matches your electric strike or magnetic lock load.
