Zhaoqing sits at the western edge of the Pearl River Delta, about 70 km upstream from Guangzhou on the West River (Xi Jiang). It is the point where the Pearl River Delta's dense manufacturing belt transitions into the less industrialized corridor toward Guangxi and Yunnan. That geographic position defines Zhaoqing's industrial character: it is close enough to the Pearl River Delta's supply chain depth to participate in it, but land costs, labor costs, and industrial land availability sit meaningfully below the core PRD cities. The result is a manufacturing base that is broadly capable but selectively specialized — strongest in auto parts and NEV components (anchored by BAIC and Great Wall Automobile plants), ceramics and building materials, aluminum fabrication, and the electronic components that serve the PRD assembly ecosystem.

The most significant industrial development in Zhaoqing over the past decade has been the establishment of OEM automobile production facilities and the supply chain that has grown around them. BAIC Group and Great Wall Automobile — the latter producing Ora and Tank brand vehicles — both operate manufacturing facilities in the city. Around these plants, a tier-1 and tier-2 supplier network has developed across the Zhaoqing Economic Development Zone and adjacent industrial areas.
