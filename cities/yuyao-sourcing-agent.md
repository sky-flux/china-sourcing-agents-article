Yuyao (余姚) sits in the heart of Ningbo prefecture, 40 minutes from Ningbo city center and 40 minutes from Ningbo-Zhoushan Port — the world's busiest port by cargo tonnage. The city is nationally recognized as China's plastics capital: the Baixi area and surrounding industrial zones form the country's largest concentration of plastics manufacturing, with annual plastics trade volume exceeding 60 billion RMB and more than 6,000 manufacturers operating across plastic products, injection molding, raw material trading, and mold-making. For electronics buyers, appliance sourcing managers, and auto parts procurement teams, Yuyao is not a secondary sourcing option — it is the primary source for a specific and important class of manufactured components.

The scale of Yuyao's plastics cluster is difficult to overstate. The Baixi plastic materials market (白泥坑塑料市场) in Yuyao's Baixi Town is the largest plastics raw material and finished goods trading hub in China, handling thousands of tons of resin transactions daily. The city hosts the China International Plastics Expo annually — one of the most significant plastics industry events in Asia — which draws manufacturers, material suppliers, and mold makers from across the country.

The depth of the cluster is what sets Yuyao apart from other plastics manufacturing zones. Within a 20-kilometer radius, you can source raw ABS, PC, PA66, and POM resin; get custom color-matched compounding; commission a multi-cavity injection mold; run production; apply surface treatment (painting, UV coating, laser etching); and assemble the finished component into a subassembly. That vertical integration within a single city reduces lead times and logistics costs that would otherwise inflate when sourcing across regions.
