---

Chongqing is China's undisputed motorcycle manufacturing capital, making it a critical hub for global procurement. The numbers are not rhetorical: in 2025 this single municipality produced 7.8 million motorcycles, representing over one-third of China's national output. Loncin, Lifan, and Zongshen — three of China's largest motorcycle manufacturers by global export volume — are all headquartered here. The downstream ecosystem includes 50+ vehicle assemblers and over 400 component suppliers within the municipality, manufacturing everything from bare engines and gearboxes to instrument clusters, lighting assemblies, and electronic fuel injection systems.

Beyond motorcycles, working with a Chongqing sourcing agent gives you access to a major four-wheel vehicle manufacturing center (Changan Auto is headquartered here), a growing furniture cluster, and — critically for global supply chain logistics — both the starting point of the Yuxinou China-Europe railway and the site of China's largest inland river port on the Yangtze.

As your dedicated sourcing agent in Chongqing, we oversee the entire procurement process, from factory identification to final quality control.
