Dongguan sits midway between Guangzhou and Shenzhen, 45 minutes from each by expressway, and that geography has made it one of China's most diversified manufacturing cities. Electronics contract manufacturing earned Dongguan its "World Factory" label, but the city's full manufacturing profile is considerably broader: it is the world's leading base for electronic toys and trendy collectibles, a significant furniture manufacturing center, and a growing hub for LED lighting. For buyers whose sourcing spans categories, Dongguan is often the most efficient single-city source in the Pearl River Delta.

For electronics buyers specifically, Dongguan delivers comparable consumer electronics assembly quality to Shenzhen at 15–25% lower cost. On a $500,000 production order, the savings pay for several factory audits and QC inspections with money to spare.

**Consumer electronics assembly** is Dongguan's primary electronics strength. The city's EMS factories handle mid-to-high volume production of Bluetooth audio products, IoT controllers, smart home hardware, portable charging devices, LED lighting electronics, and a broad range of accessory categories. Factories in Chang'an and Houjie districts routinely run 50,000–500,000 unit production orders for US and European buyers. Foxconn and BYD Electronics both operate significant assembly bases here, and their supply chain ecosystems have anchored hundreds of second-tier manufacturers in the city.
