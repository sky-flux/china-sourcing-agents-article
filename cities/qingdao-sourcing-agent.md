Engineer-led sourcing in Qingdao. Haier & Hisense supply chain for appliances, smart-home electronics, and industrial mfg. Top-10 container port.

---

Qingdao is Shandong Province's economic capital and the headquarters of Haier — the world's largest home appliance company by unit volume — and Hisense, the world's third-largest TV manufacturer. The supply ecosystem both companies built over four decades spans 8,000+ manufacturers producing control electronics, motor assemblies, sensors, display modules, and smart home integration hardware concentrated within 80 km of central Qingdao. For buyers sourcing home appliances, appliance sub-assemblies, smart home control hardware, or large-format display modules, Qingdao offers supply depth and unit pricing that Shenzhen and Shanghai cannot match in these specific categories.

**Home appliance electronics and OEM components** are the defining Qingdao category. The Haier supply chain — primarily in the Laoshan, Chengyang, and Laixi industrial zones — produces inverter control boards for compressors, BLDC motor assemblies for washing machines, smart home control panels, NTC thermistor sensors, and large-format plastic enclosures for appliance housings (0.3m–1.2m). For buyers building OEM white goods, sourcing components for the appliance repair and upgrade market, or developing products that integrate into existing appliance form factors, Qingdao provides component diversity and pricing that sourcing through Guangdong intermediaries cannot replicate.

**Display modules and commercial display hardware** from Hisense's supply chain are the second major category. Hisense's Qingdao facilities produce commercial-grade LCD modules from 32" to 98", feeding a downstream supply cluster that makes custom touch-integrated display assemblies, LED backlight modules, and driver board sets for industrial HMI, digital signage, conference room systems, and retail kiosks. Buyers currently sourcing commercial displays from Shenzhen distributors typically see 20–30% unit cost reduction on a delivered-duty-paid basis when sourcing direct from Qingdao, plus logistics advantages from Qingdao's direct Europe shipping services.
