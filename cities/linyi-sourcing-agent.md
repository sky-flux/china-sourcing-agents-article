Instead, Linyi is northern China's largest wholesale and distribution center — a city whose commercial identity is built around Linyi Commercial City (临沂商城), a market complex whose annual transaction volume consistently ranks it among the highest in China by total trade value.

For overseas buyers, understanding what Linyi specializes in — and where it sits in the value chain — is the first step to using it effectively.
