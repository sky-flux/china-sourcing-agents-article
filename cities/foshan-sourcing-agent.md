Foshan is one of China's most productive manufacturing cities — and one of the most misunderstood by foreign buyers who only associate it with small appliances. The city actually holds three globally dominant manufacturing positions: the world's largest single furniture cluster, the world's largest architectural ceramics hub, and China's home appliance capital. If you are sourcing furniture, tiles, sanitary ware, home appliances, smart home hardware, or building materials, Foshan's supplier density is hard to match anywhere on the planet.

**Furniture** is Foshan's largest manufacturing sector by volume and global reach. The Lecong-Longjiang corridor in Shunde district is the single largest furniture production and wholesale cluster in the world. Longjiang township alone hosts over 1,200 furniture factories, and Lecong's China Furniture and Decoration City (中国家具装饰材料城) functions as the global wholesale benchmark for wood, upholstered, and decorative furniture. Categories we actively source from this cluster: solid wood furniture, MDF case goods, upholstered sofas and beds, outdoor rattan and aluminum furniture, office furniture, hotel furniture FF&E, and custom private-label collections.

**Architectural ceramics and tiles** represent Foshan's second pillar. Chancheng district and the surrounding Nanshan industrial zone produce roughly 60% of China's total ceramic tile output — including floor tile, wall tile, large-format porcelain slabs (up to 1,600 x 3,200 mm), mosaic tile, and exterior facade tile. Foshan is the reference market for tile pricing and technology globally. Buyers sourcing for real estate developments, hospitality refits, or tile retail programs will find more factory optionality here than anywhere else in the world.
