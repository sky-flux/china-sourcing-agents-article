Major German DIY retailers (Bauhaus, Hornbach, OBI) and hardware distributors increasingly require GS as a buyer qualification — without it, you cannot get shelf space when sourcing from an **angle grinder manufacturer in China**. EU Directive 2002/44/EC (Physical Agents — Vibration) requires employers to monitor workers' daily vibration exposure.
