Shantou is eastern Guangdong's primary coastal industrial city, located 470 km east of Guangzhou. It is one of China's original Special Economic Zones, designated in 1981, with a deep export manufacturing history. The city's global manufacturing identity is defined overwhelmingly by one category: toys. Chenghai District, a sub-district within Shantou, is recognized as the world's toy capital by production volume — approximately 70% of the world's plastic toys are made within its boundaries. For any buyer sourcing plastic toys, festive and Christmas supplies, or decorative novelty products, Shantou is the primary sourcing destination in China, not a secondary option. Our sourcing services include supplier identification, factory audits, and production monitoring for toy, festive supply, and plastics programs in Chenghai.

**Plastic toys** are Shantou's core competency and global identity. Chenghai District hosts more than 50,000 toy-related companies — manufacturers, component suppliers, mold shops, packaging factories, and logistics providers — concentrated in a zone roughly 20 km across. The category range is comprehensive: RC vehicles and drones, plastic action figures and collectibles, educational toys, building block sets, board games with plastic components, inflatable products, toy vehicles, and dolls. Electronic integration in Chenghai toys has advanced significantly since 2015 — Bluetooth-connected toys, microcontroller-based educational devices, and app-controlled RC products are now routine production for factories that once only made purely mechanical products.

**Christmas and holiday festive supplies** represent a category where Shantou's dominance is comparable to its toy position. Approximately one-third of all Christmas goods sold in the United States originate in Chenghai — plastic Christmas ornaments, novelty figurines, light-up decorations, artificial tree components, gift packaging, and seasonal tableware. The same factories that produce toys often run festive supply lines using the same injection molding and decorating capabilities. For importers building Christmas product programs, Chenghai is not one option among many; it is where the category is made.
